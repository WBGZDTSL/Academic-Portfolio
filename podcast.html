What You Will Find Here

These essays distill ongoing research into narrative form. Each piece pairs short visual sketches with rigorous explanations, so readers can grasp the intuition and the evidence without relying on audio or interviews.

Topics range from model evaluation and reproducibility to reflective essays on research practice. I focus on clarity, reproducible references, and a careful record of what remains uncertain.

New articles are published monthly and linked with relevant reading lists for deeper study.
