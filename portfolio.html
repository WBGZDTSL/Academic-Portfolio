ACL 2024 · Conference Paper

Attention Mechanisms in Large-Scale Language Models: A Comprehensive Survey

A systematic review of attention variants across 80+ transformer architectures, with a unified taxonomy and empirical benchmarks on five NLP tasks. We identify convergent design principles and unresolved trade-offs between efficiency and expressiveness.

EMNLP 2023 · Conference Paper

Cross-Modal Representation Learning for Scientific Document Understanding

A contrastive pre-training framework that jointly encodes text, figures, and tables from scientific papers. Achieves state-of-the-art results on three scientific retrieval benchmarks, with particularly strong gains on figure–caption alignment tasks.

Chen, A. & Martinez, J. · EMNLP 2023

NeurIPS 2023 · Workshop Paper

Efficient Fine-Tuning Strategies for Domain-Specific Foundation Models

Comparative analysis of parameter-efficient tuning methods applied to biomedical and legal NLP. We find that the optimal strategy varies substantially by task type and data volume, and release a decision framework to guide practitioners.

Chen, A., Kim, S., & Okafor, T. · NeurIPS Efficient NLP Workshop 2023

ICLR 2022 · Conference Paper

Graph Neural Networks for Knowledge Graph Completion in Low-Resource Settings

We introduce a relational graph attention network that achieves competitive performance on knowledge graph completion with fewer than 10% of the triples used by existing baselines, enabling deployment in specialized domains with limited annotation.

Chen, A. & Lee, H. · ICLR 2022
