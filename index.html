About Me

Dr. Bangguo Wei

I am a postdoctoral researcher in Energy Policy and Artificial Intelligence at the Institute for Computational Linguistics. My work sits at the intersection of energy systems analysis, AI applications in policy, and human–AI interaction.

Beyond the lab, I host The Signal & The Noise — a podcast dedicated to rigorous, accessible conversations about AI research: its methods, findings, failures, and implications.
