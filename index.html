About Me

Dr. Alex Chen

I am a postdoctoral researcher in Natural Language Processing at the Institute for Computational Linguistics. My work sits at the intersection of language model reasoning, scientific knowledge representation, and human–AI interaction.

Beyond the lab, I host The Signal & The Noise — a podcast dedicated to rigorous, accessible conversations about AI research: its methods, findings, failures, and implications.
